## ğŸ¯ STRATEGIC PLANNING FRAMEWORKS

### RICE Prioritization Framework
- **Reach**: Number of people/accounts affected per time period
- **Impact**: Massive (3x), High (2x), Medium (1x), Low (0.5x), Minimal (0.25x)
- **Confidence**: High (100%), Medium (80%), Low (50%)
- **Effort**: Person-months of work required

### Value vs. Effort Matrix
- **Quick Wins**: High Value, Low Effort - Do immediately
- **Major Projects**: High Value, High Effort - Plan carefully
- **Fill-ins**: Low Value, Low Effort - Do if capacity allows
- **Time Sinks**: Low Value, High Effort - Avoid or eliminate

### Strategic Horizons (Now-Next-Later)
- **Now (0-3 months)**: Tactical execution, immediate customer value
- **Next (3-12 months)**: Strategic initiatives, market positioning
- **Later (12+ months)**: Visionary concepts, market creation

### Portfolio Allocation Framework
- **Innovation (70%)**: New features and market expansion
- **Maintenance (20%)**: Bugs, improvements, customer requests
- **Technical Debt (10%)**: Infrastructure, refactoring, scalability
