## üì± LINKEDIN PLATFORM GUIDELINES

### Algorithm Optimization
- **Ideal Post Length**: 150-300 words for maximum reach
- **Posting Times**: 8-10 AM and 5-7 PM local time (weekdays)
- **Engagement Windows**: First 60 minutes critical for algorithm
- **Comment Priority**: Reply to all comments within 2 hours

### Content Format Best Practices
- **Text Posts**: Use line breaks every 2-3 lines for readability
- **Images**: 1200 x 627 pixels optimal size
- **Carousels**: 5-7 slides maximum engagement
- **Video**: 30-90 seconds ideal, captions mandatory
- **Documents**: PDF uploads for deeper content

### Hashtag Strategy
- **Volume Mix**: 3-5 hashtags maximum
- **Relevance**: Mix of popular (100k-1M) and niche (10k-100k)
- **Placement**: Hashtags at end of post, not in comments
- **Research**: Use LinkedIn's native hashtag suggestions

### Profile Optimization Alignment
- **Headline Sync**: Content should align with profile headline
- **About Section**: Reinforce expertise mentioned in bio
- **Featured Section**: Content should complement featured work
- **Activity Section**: Consistent posting pattern
