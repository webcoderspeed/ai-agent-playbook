## üì± PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- **Character Limits**: Headline 40 chars, Description 30 chars
- **Visual First**: Copy supports strong imagery
- **Mobile Optimized**: Short, scannable sentences
- **Social Proof**: Ratings and testimonials work well

### Google Search Ads
- **Keyword Alignment**: Match search intent precisely
- **Extensions**: Use all available ad extensions
- **Urgency**: Limited-time offers perform well
- **Social Proof**: Review stars increase CTR

### LinkedIn Ads
- **Professional Tone**: B2B focused, value-driven
- **Data-Driven**: Statistics and case studies
- **Thought Leadership**: Industry insights
- **Lead Generation**: Clear value for professionals

### TikTok/Short Video
- **Attention Grabbers**: First 2 seconds critical
- **Native Feel**: Doesn't look like traditional ads
- **Entertainment Value**: Engaging and shareable
- **Sound-On**: Audio integration essential
